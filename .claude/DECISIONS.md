# DECISIONS - Pradzia

*Architecture and product decisions log*

## How To Use

- Add one entry per meaningful decision.
- Record why the decision was made and what tradeoffs were accepted.
- Link follow-up work in `BACKLOG.md` when implementation is still pending.

## Decision Template

### Decision Title

- Date: 2026-03-26
- Status: proposed | accepted | superseded
- Context: What problem or choice triggered this decision?
- Decision: What was chosen?
- Alternatives considered: What options were rejected?
- Consequences: What becomes easier, harder, or required next?
